The Science

Clinically Studied Ingredients, Backed by Real Research

Ingredient 1 of 4 • GLP-1 & Appetite Support

Decaffeinated Green Coffee Extract

400 mg (standardized to 200 mg chlorogenic acid)

Chlorogenic acid — the polyphenol responsible for green coffee's effects — has been studied in multiple human clinical trials as one of the more researched natural GLP-1 supplements, without the stimulant load of caffeine.

  • In a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial in 43 adults with metabolic syndrome, 800 mg/day of green coffee extract for 8 weeks led to a significant reduction in appetite scores vs. placebo (p = 0.01)

  • The same 8-week trial showed participants taking green coffee extract lost meaningfully more weight than the placebo group, alongside improved fasting blood glucose and insulin resistance markers

  • In a crossover trial of 11 healthy men, decaffeinated coffee significantly increased GLP-1 levels (p < 0.01) in the two hours after drinking it — while the caffeinated version showed no such effect, isolating chlorogenic acid as the active compound

  • A separate randomized crossover study found decaffeinated coffee significantly reduced hunger (p < 0.05) and raised levels of PYY, a natural satiety hormone, for up to 90 minutes — with no effect from caffeine alone

🔎 Key ingredient: Decaffeinated Green Coffee Extract (400 mg) — randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial, n=43, 8 weeks (Roshan et al., 2018, British Journal of Nutrition)

Ingredient 2 of 4 • Satiety & Weight Management

Hibiscus Extract

250 mg (standardized to ≥9% anthocyanins)

Hibiscus has a long history of traditional use for metabolic wellness, and is one of the more clinically explored natural sources of GLP-1 support — particularly for satiety and healthy body composition.

  • In an 8-week randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled crossover trial in 33 overweight or obese adults, a hibiscus-containing polyphenol blend significantly increased satiety quotient scores vs. placebo (p < 0.0001)

  • Participants in that same trial spontaneously ate fewer calories per meal (774 kcal vs. 850 kcal on placebo, p < 0.004) — a real-world eating-behavior outcome, not just a lab marker

  • In a separate randomized controlled trial, 450 mg/day of hibiscus extract led to a significant reduction in body weight (88.52 kg → 87.28 kg, p < 0.008) and BMI (p < 0.009)

  • A systematic review and meta-analysis of 10 RCTs (453 total participants) found 4 of the 10 studies showed significant reductions in body weight, fat mass, and BMI with hibiscus supplementation

🔎 Key ingredient: Hibiscus Extract (250 mg) — 8-week randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled crossover trial, n=33 (Serna et al., 2022, European Journal of Nutrition)

Ingredient 3 of 4 • Metabolic & Body Composition Support

Curcumin Extract

500 mg (≥95% curcuminoids)

Curcumin is one of the most extensively studied plant compounds for metabolic wellness, with a meta-analysis spanning nearly 3,000 participants backing its role in healthy body composition — making it a well-documented choice among GLP-1 natural supplements.

  • A meta-analysis of 50 randomized controlled trials (2,879 total participants) found curcumin supplementation significantly reduced BMI (-0.24 kg/m²), body weight (-0.59 kg), and waist circumference (-1.32 cm) vs. placebo

  • In a 60-day randomized controlled trial, a bioavailability-enhanced curcumin formulation (with piperine, paired here with UNALTERED's own black pepper extract) produced a total weight loss of 6.70% in participants who had plateaued on diet and lifestyle changes alone

  • That same trial showed meaningful reductions in waist circumference (up to 4.14%) and hip circumference (up to 2.51%), with excellent tolerability and no dropouts

  • The meta-analysis confirmed that bioavailability-enhanced curcumin formulations — like those paired with piperine — showed greater effects than standard curcumin alone

🔎 Key ingredient: Curcumin Extract (500 mg) — meta-analysis of 50 RCTs, n=2,879 (Unhapipatpong et al., 2023, American Journal of Clinical Nutrition)

Ingredient 4 of 4 • Absorption Support

Black Pepper Extract

10 mg (≥95% piperine)

A formula is only as effective as what your body actually absorbs. Black pepper extract is included specifically to enhance the bioavailability of curcumin — helping your body make the most of every dose.

  • A meta-analysis of 50 randomized controlled trials (2,879 participants) found bioavailability-enhanced curcumin formulations — including those paired with piperine — produced greater effects than standard curcumin alone

  • In a 60-day randomized controlled trial, curcumin combined with 8 mg/day of piperine for enhanced absorption helped participants achieve a 6.70% total weight loss after plateauing on diet and lifestyle changes alone

  • Piperine is one of the most well-established bioavailability enhancers in supplement science, helping more curcuminoids stay active in the bloodstream

🔎 Key ingredient: Black Pepper Extract (10 mg) — included as a bioavailability enhancer for curcumin; supporting data from Unhapipatpong et al., 2023, American Journal of Clinical Nutrition (meta-analysis of 50 RCTs, n=2,879)

Can a supplement really compare to prescription GLP-1 drugs like Ozempic?

No, and we want to be upfront about that. Prescription GLP-1 medications work by directly mimicking the GLP-1 hormone and are a different category entirely. UNALTERED GLP-1 Support is a nutritional supplement designed to support your body's own natural GLP-1 function and appetite-regulating processes as part of a healthy lifestyle, not to replace or replicate a prescription drug.

As with any supplement, we recommend a quick check-in with your doctor if you're pregnant, nursing, or managing a chronic health condition.
